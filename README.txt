Java program used to iterate over The Lord of The Rings novel determining:

1. Total number of words in the novel
2. Total number of unique words in the novel
3. List of the 10 most frequent words
4. List of words that appear 64 times
5. Comparing the speed of ArrayList contains, ArrayList binary search
and simpleHashSet contains search for words in Lord of the Rings that are
not in the dictionary
6. A check for which of the 18 characters has the word "ring" appear at most
42 words before or 42 words after their name is mentioned
